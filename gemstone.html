Understanding Planetary Stones

Planetary stones are gemstones that are traditionally associated with the planets of our solar system. These associations come from ancient astrological and metaphysical traditions. Each stone is believed to resonate with the energy of its corresponding planet and can be used in meditation, healing practices, or as personal talismans.
