Core Personality

Element: Fire

Quality: Cardinal

Ruling Planet: Mars

Symbol: The Ram

Aries are natural leaders, filled with energy and enthusiasm. They approach life with initiative and courage.

Strengths

  • Courageous and determined
  • Confident and enthusiastic
  • Optimistic and honest
  • Passionate and intelligent
  • Pioneering spirit

Weaknesses

  • Impatient and moody
  • Short-tempered and impulsive
  • Aggressive and selfish
  • Argumentative and reckless

Love & Relationships

Aries are passionate lovers who enjoy the thrill of the chase. They need partners who can keep up with their energy.

Best Matches: Leo, Sagittarius, Gemini

Challenges: Can be impatient in relationships

Career & Money

Thrive in leadership positions and competitive environments. Natural entrepreneurs.

Ideal Careers: Military, sports, entrepreneurship, surgery

Money Style: Impulsive spenders, good at making money
